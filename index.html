Our Services

Corrugated box

A corrugated box is a durable and lightweight packaging solution made from fluted paperboard, designed to protect goods during transit. Ideal for shipping, storage, and product safety across various industries.

Duplex Corrugated box

A Duplex Corrugated Box is made with high-strength duplex board and fluted layers, offering enhanced durability and a smooth surface for quality printing. It is ideal for packaging heavy or high-end products requiring both protection and visual appeal.

Four color Corrugated box

A Four Color Corrugated Box uses advanced CMYK printing to deliver vibrant, high-quality graphics on durable corrugated material. Perfect for branded packaging that combines strength with visual appeal, making it ideal for retail display and promotional use.
